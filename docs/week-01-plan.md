The bridge from the growth playbook to Monday. This is week 1 of the 12-week reach->convert
arc, so the job this week is **reach + authority + commenting**, not selling. Pillar mix leans
PM (summit runway) with one Strait Up Growth and one iGaming day so the account reads as Stuart,
not a summit feed. Post copy is in `docs/pillar-posts-draft-2026-07-19.md` (pillars) and the
NEXTPredict gallery (PM). Nothing here sends or publishes; Stuart posts by hand.

## The daily rhythm (every weekday, ~40 min total)
1. **Post** (see the day below). LinkedIn: hook in the first two lines, link in the first
   comment, reply to every comment for the first 90 minutes. X: strong standalone first line.
2. **Engage, ~25 min** (the biggest growth lever): 5-8 substantive comments on the day's targets
   below. Add a real point, never "great post". This borrows their audience and builds the
   relationships that convert later.

## Rules that keep this credible (from the playbook)
- No link in the post body (first comment only). No hype words. No em dashes.
- NEXTPredict/PM posts stay strictly in category vocabulary (no betting/gambling). The iGaming
  post may use sportsbook vocabulary; it is a NEXT.io/Stuart-personal post, not NEXTPredict.
- One ask per post. This week the "ask" is a question or an invitation to reply, never a ticket sell.
