# Intel Triage and Verification

## What Stuart needs from intel

Do not default to long summaries. Turn the signal into a next action:

- Post
- Comment
- Repost
- DM
- Email
- Sales handoff
- Speaker lead
- Sponsor lead
- Monitor
- Ignore

When the user says next, give one item at a time.

## High-priority intel

- CFTC movement
- State versus federal conflict
- Sports contract litigation
- World Cup volume and retention
- Major operator launches
- Institutional trading
- Market making
- Hedge funds and banks
- Compliance
- MNPI
- Surveillance
- Insider trading
- Talent movement
- Data
- Settlement
- Oracle infrastructure
- Media
- Brand risk
- Corporate exposure
- Consumer protection

## Medium-priority intel

- Useful founder posts
- UX debates
- Sportsbook comparison threads
- Analyst takes worth expanding
- Niche infrastructure companies with credible signals

## Usually ignore

- Generic SEO content
- Token promotions
- Clone-script content
- Low-signal prize pools
- Affiliate best-app lists unless they reveal distribution or category positioning
- Random retail profit-and-loss posts unless they expose market mechanics
- Unverified hyperbole

## Verification rules

Verify before publishing claims involving:

- Numbers
- Market volume
- Active users
- Legal action
- Litigation
- Court rulings
- Regulation
- Tax rates
- Valuation
- Funding
- Partnerships
- Named company launches
- Speaker or sponsor involvement
- Contract settlement disputes

## Source hierarchy

Prefer:

1. Court or regulatory documents
2. Company announcements and official product pages
3. Academic papers and primary research
4. Reputable news outlets
5. Specialist industry press
6. Screenshots and social posts for initial triage
7. Reddit and community discussion for sentiment or leads, not confirmation

## Language for unverified claims

- reported
- appears to
- according to the post
- the chart suggests
- if this number is right
- the direction matters more than the exact number

## Metrics discipline

Always define what a number means:

- Notional volume
- Calendar-month volume
- Rolling seven-day volume
- Open interest
- Number of contracts
- Number of active traders
- Revenue
- Handle
- Deposits

Do not compare unlike metrics as though they are equivalent.

## Legal discipline

Do not claim a court ruling creates a definitive national rule unless it genuinely does. Distinguish:

- Temporary order
- Preliminary injunction
- Final judgment
- Appeal
- Agency filing
- Proposed rule
- Final rule
- State action
- Federal action

## Screenshot discipline

A screenshot is evidence that someone posted or displayed a claim. It is not proof that the underlying claim is true.
