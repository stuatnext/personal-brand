# Final Checklist

Before delivering or publishing, check every item below.

## Voice

- Does this sound like Stuart rather than a generic analyst?
- Is the opening human or unexpected?
- Is there a clear commercial read?
- Are contractions used naturally?
- Is the humour understated rather than forced?
- Is uncertainty honest where needed?

## Rhythm

- Are paragraphs natural and readable?
- Have theatrical one-line breaks been avoided?
- Is repetition removed?
- Is the post tighter than the source material?

## Banned habits

- No em dashes
- No not just X but Y construction
- No game changer
- No exciting times ahead
- No fascinating development
- No the angle
- No the bit that stands out
- No the part I keep coming back to
- No this highlights
- No broader trend
- No consultant-style throat-clearing

## Accuracy

- Are numbers verified and correctly defined?
- Are legal claims properly qualified?
- Are partnerships and company developments sourced?
- Are screenshot claims treated as claims rather than facts?
- Are inferences clearly presented as inferences?

## Platform

- Are there three distinct versions for social topics?
- Is personal LinkedIn developed enough?
- Is the Forum version practitioner-led with one clear question?
- Is X sharp, timely and precise?

## NEXTPredict

- Is any mention natural rather than forced?
- Does the wording preserve relationship sensitivities?
- Have unconfirmed speakers, sponsors or partners been avoided?

## Final test

Would a reader think Stuart is paying attention to the right signal and worth speaking to?
