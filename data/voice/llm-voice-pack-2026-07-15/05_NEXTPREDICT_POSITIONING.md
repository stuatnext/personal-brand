# NEXTPredict Positioning

## Core statement

NEXTPredict is a category-formation room for prediction markets.

## Event

The World's Prediction Markets Summit
New York City
22 to 23 October 2026
Convene, Hudson Yards

## Audience

- Exchanges
- Operators
- Trading firms
- Brokers
- Investors
- Regulators
- Founders
- Suppliers
- Infrastructure companies
- Compliance leaders
- Data providers
- Media
- Analysts

## What the event is about

- Market structure
- Responsible scale
- Regulation
- Liquidity
- Compliance
- Settlement
- Data
- Distribution
- Institutional participation
- Sports and entertainment adoption
- Brand and consumer trust

## What it is not

- A generic crypto conference
- A sportsbook product launch
- A hype-led retail-trading event
- A forced sales CTA attached to every post

## Desired impression

The room should feel like the place serious people need to be if prediction markets touch their business.

## Approved soft references

- That's exactly the kind of conversation NEXTPredict is being built around.
- This is why the room matters.
- If prediction markets are even close to your world, this is probably one of those rooms worth being in.
- The opportunity is obvious. The architecture is not.

## Relationship sensitivities

Kalshi leadership is involved with NEXTPredict. Avoid anti-Kalshi or anti-Polymarket framing when discussing category concentration, liquidity or leadership.

Prefer:

- category leaders
- early leaders create liquidity
- market formation
- what develops around that momentum
- the surrounding infrastructure layer

Do not imply Kalshi or Polymarket are confirmed speakers, sponsors or partners unless directly verified.

## Commercial priority

Credibility first. Ticket sales, sponsor leads and speaker leads matter, but the content should earn attention before asking for anything.
