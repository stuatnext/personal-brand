# NEXTPredict Claude Intelligence Pack

**Coverage period:** 17-18 July 2026  
**Prepared for:** Stuart Crowley / NEXTPredict  
**Purpose:** LLM-readable intelligence, lead generation, editorial planning and conference development.

## What is included

1. `01_FULL_COMBINED_BRIEF.md` - Consolidated editorial and commercial intelligence brief.
2. `02_SIGNALS_MASTER.md` - Prioritised signal table with confidence, action and NEXTPredict relevance.
3. `03_LEADS_MASTER.md` - Speaker, sponsor, media, regulatory and institutional leads.
4. `04_CONTENT_AND_ACTIONS.md` - Recommended editorial sequence, content ideas and outreach priorities.
5. `05_SOURCE_INDEX.md` - Source URLs grouped by theme.
6. `06_VERIFICATION_AND_GAPS.md` - Corrections, caveats and claims requiring further confirmation.
7. `07_SIGNALS.jsonl` - One structured signal per line for machine ingestion.
8. `08_LEADS.jsonl` - One structured lead per line for machine ingestion.
9. `09_RAW_ADDITIONAL_INTEL.md` - Lower-priority and niche material retained for recall.
10. `10_CLAUDE_OPERATING_PROMPT.md` - Suggested instructions for using this pack in Claude.
11. `manifest.json` - File manifest and schema notes.

## Recommended Claude workflow

- Start with `01_FULL_COMBINED_BRIEF.md` for context.
- Use `07_SIGNALS.jsonl` and `08_LEADS.jsonl` for structured analysis.
- Use `06_VERIFICATION_AND_GAPS.md` before drafting public claims.
- Use `04_CONTENT_AND_ACTIONS.md` when producing posts, DMs, emails or NEXTPredict programming ideas.

## Editorial guardrails

- Separate verified facts from inference and social-source claims.
- Do not repeat dramatic wording unless it is directly supported by a primary source.
- Treat market odds and volumes as timestamped observations.
- Do not describe potential platform distribution as confirmed user adoption.
- Do not describe paper-trading results as executable profitability.
- Keep Stuart commercially sharp, curious and credible. Avoid guru language and forced NEXTPredict promotion.
