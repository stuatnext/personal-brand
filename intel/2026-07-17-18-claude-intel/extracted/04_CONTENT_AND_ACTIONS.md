## High-value LinkedIn ideas

### 1. The infrastructure race

DraftKings, UDX, Jump, Moreton and Pascal show the category moving from consumer acquisition into exchange infrastructure, professional trading and capital formation.

### 2. Who counts as an insider?

Compare sports, reality television, clinical trials, weather, corporate events and government communications. Each creates a different insider population.

### 3. Contract design is compliance infrastructure

Use the five-minute Bitcoin research, USD.AI dispute and FlightAware issue to show how settlement windows, definitions and data rights shape integrity.

### 4. Europe is choosing sides

Contrast France's block with Malta and Gibraltar's attempts to build licensing routes.

### 5. The incumbent-pressure signal

Entain mentioning prediction markets may be more commercially revealing than another record-volume headline.
